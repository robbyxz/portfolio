PROJECTS

Spam Email Detection & Classification

Summary:

Spam messages expose users to phishing and fraud risks. This project focuses on building an automated email and SMS filtering pipeline to classify messages as either legitimate ("Ham") or "Spam," balancing high recall with precision.

Methodology:

  • Preprocessing: Text cleaning, tokenization, and sequence padding.
  • Architecture: Comparative analysis of Multinomial Naive Bayes, Bernoulli Naive Bayes, and a Deep Learning Recurrent Neural Network (RNN).
  • Optimization: Used Embedding layers and Dropout regularization to improve model generalization.

Business Impact:

This automated system reduces phishing exposure and lowers operational costs associated with manual content moderation while maintaining high precision for legitimate business communications.
